Our Story

When the two first met back in December of 2009 Abdur Jabbar was an active duty Air Force member and Keshia Ashley was a recent graduate of LSUS with a bachelor's degree in Biology. Keshia and Abdur both knew a mutual friend that wanted both of them to meet so that Abdur would have a date for his upcoming squadron's Christmas party. After exchanging phone numbers and friending each other on Facebook they decided to meet at Chili's on December 12, 2009 in order to get to know each other better before attending the Christmas party. Abdur was estatic after meeting Keshia and soon afterwards asked her to be his girlfriend on January 7, 2009. The couple married one year later and has lived together happily ever since!
